Stripe’s Head of Design Katie Dill is one of the most influential designers in Silicon Valley. Before joining Stripe she
led the design team at Lyft and was Head of Experience Design at Airbnb. Throughout her career she’s leveraged the power
of great design to help build a few of the most successful companies in the world. So what’s the secret to her success?
For Katie it’s all about focusing on the details, and making sure that every pixel counts.

In this episode YC’s Aaron Epstein and Katie Dill review startup landing pages with a focus on creating sites that
builds trust, convert customers and brings some joy to the user experience.
