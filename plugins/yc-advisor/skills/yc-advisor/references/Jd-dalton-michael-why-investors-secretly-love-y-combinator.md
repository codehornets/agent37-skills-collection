Wrap Up

It's incredible how many very successful YC startups there are. Just look at the [**YC Top Revenue**\\
**list**](https://www.ycombinator.com/topcompanies/revenue).

Almost all of those companies raised their first rounds from investors that saw them debut at YC Demo Day. YC is
undeniably an integral part of the venture capital ecosystem, helping to shine the first spotlight on many of the
companies that go on to be featured high up in a huge font on the top VC portfolio pages.

But how do investors _really_ feel about YC? In this episode, Michael and Dalton dig in!
