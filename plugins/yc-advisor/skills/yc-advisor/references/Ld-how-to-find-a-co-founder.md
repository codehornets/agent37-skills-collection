Summary

Building a startup and trying to break into an established market is hard, especially if you’re trying to do it alone!
That’s why it’s critical to find the right co-founder for the journey. In this episode of Startup School, YC Group
Partner Harj Taggar explains why you need a co-founder, when to bring them on, where you can find one and how to
maintain the relationship.
