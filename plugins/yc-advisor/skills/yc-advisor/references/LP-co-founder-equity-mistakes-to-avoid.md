In order to get your startup off the ground it's critical to keep your co-founders motivated. One of the best ways to do
that is to figure out a fair co-founder equity split. In this episode of Startup School, YC Group Partner Michael Seibel
explains the ins and outs of co-founder equity, why it's important to be generous with that equity, and how to avoid bad
advice that can lead to co-founder breakups.
