Garry sits down with Ryan Petersen, CEO of Flexport.

Founded in 2013, Flexport combines powerful technology with physical transportation infrastructure to modernize freight
forwarding. Today, nearly 10,000 companies ranging from emerging brands to Fortune 500s use Flexport to move nearly $19
Billion dollars worth of merchandise across 112 countries.
