# Tips for technical startup founders

**Author:** Diana Hu
**Type:** Video
**URL:** https://www.ycombinator.com/library/Is-tips-for-technical-startup-founders
**YouTube ID:** rP7bpYsfa6Q

---

YC Group Partner Diana Hu was the CTO of her YC startup Escher Reality, which was acquired by Niantic (makers of Pokemon
Go). She shares her advice for being a technical founder at the earliest stages - including topics like how to ship an
MVP fast, how to deal with technology choices and technical debt, and how and when to hire an engineering team.
